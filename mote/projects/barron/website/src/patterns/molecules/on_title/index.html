On Title Base

Adds an On Device atom above a heading. Treated as an inline block to ensure that the line never extends past the length of the text. It has two modifiers, both of which can be used simultaneously. 
            
                
{% render_element element.aspect.molecules.on_title %}

On Title Inverted

By adding OnTitle--inverted the colors flip to make it easier to read on a dark background. 
            
                
{% render_element element.aspect.molecules.on_title.inverted %}

On Title Massive

By adding OnTitle--massive we get a larger text, as seen in the Hero Banner.
